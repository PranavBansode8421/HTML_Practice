Warren Buffett

Warren Buffett, often called the Oracle of Omaha for his investment wisdom.

Early Life

Warren Buffett was born on August 30, 1930 in Omaha, Nebraska. His interest in business and investing developed at a young age.

Investment Strategy

Buffett is known for his long-term investment strategies. He believes in the power of value investingâ€”focusing on companies with strong fundamentals.

He has often said: It is far better to buy a wonderful company at a fair price than a fair company at a wonderful price.

Philanthropy

In 2006, Warren Buffett pledged to give away 99% of his wealth to philanthropic causes, much of which will go to the BMGF. His focus on charity has inspired many.

Famous Quotes

Warren Buffett has shared timeless wisdom over the years. As quoted by the media:

"Rule No. 1: Never lose money. Rule No. 2: Never forget Rule No. 1."

Personal Life

Warren Buffett is well known for his simple lifestyle. Despite his immense wealth, he still lives in his Omaha home, which he bought in 1958 for $31,500.

His favorite book, The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham, helped shape his investment philosophy.
